RangeTube plays a YouTube video on loop between two points you choose. Paste a URL,
drag the dual-handle slider to mark a start and end, and the clip repeats within that
range — handy for practising a passage of music, drilling a tutorial step, or looping
a favourite moment without touching the original video.
